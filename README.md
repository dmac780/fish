# Fish with Gun (demo)

[Play **Fish with Gun** in your browser](https://dmac780.github.io/fish/)

Fish with Gun is a twin-stick shooter where you play as a fish, with a gun.

You can also use bullet-time by holding spacebar after building up enough kills. 

Choose between 3 different fish. 

Survive 30 waves of enemies/bosses and defeat the evil that lurks below.

## Controls
- Forward - **W**
- Left - **A**
- Right - **S**
- Down - **D**
- Reload - **R**
- Shoot - **Left Click**
- Bullet-Time - **spacebar**
- Pause - **ESC**

Customizable inputs, full-screen mode, audio volume controls, fun weeby UI aesthetic.

### This is technically a DEMO version of the game. 

The full version of Fish with Gun is planned to have an indepth story and play more like a shoot-em-up/bullet-hell with a full campaign mode, unlockable characters/items/levels, and more wacky boss fights. Spritesheet animations, living backgrounds, controller support, and persistant data storage are also on the docket.
